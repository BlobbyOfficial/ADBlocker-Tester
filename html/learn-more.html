Learn More About AdBlocker Tester

Welcome to the AdBlocker Tester! This website allows you to test how well your ad blocker works by simulating hundreds of ads and tracking ad interactions.

What is AdBlocker Tester?

AdBlocker Tester is a tool designed to help users evaluate the effectiveness of their ad blockers. It simulates a variety of ad types on a web page, including banner ads, popups, sponsored content, and more. By testing with this tool, you can see how well your ad blocker detects and blocks various forms of online advertising.

How to Use the Tester

  1. Visit the AdBlocker Tester page.
  2. Adjust the settings to choose how many ads you'd like to be displayed per minute.
  3. Click the "Start Spam" button to begin the ad spam.
  4. Observe how many ads your ad blocker successfully blocks and how many ads get through.
  5. If you'd like, click on the "Learn More" button to see additional details about the testing process.

How It Works

AdBlocker Tester works by creating fake ad elements in the page using images and content that resemble real online ads. The ads are dynamically generated and inserted at a rapid rate, mimicking real-world ad serving behavior.

These ads do not lead to real external sites and do not contain any harmful or malicious content. They are purely for testing how well your ad blocker performs.

Technical Details

AdBlocker Tester uses JavaScript to simulate the display of various ad types. The website includes several files:

  • index.html - The main page where ads are displayed.
  • style.css - The styling for the page and ads.
  • script.js - The script responsible for ad spamming and interaction tracking.
  • tracker.js - A script that logs user interactions with the ads.
  • adsense-fake.js - A simulation of an ad network like Google AdSense.

Disclaimer

This site is intended purely for educational purposes. It simulates online ads and allows users to test the performance of their ad blockers. We do not collect or transmit any user data, and the simulated ads do not lead to any external sites or ads. No data is sent to external servers.
